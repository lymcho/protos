Interactions

Panel Open/Close


Clicking on the arrow carot will open and close panel universally.The carrot icon will change based on open/close status.

The open/close should not intefere with panel states.(e.g. if on RD, closing panel will not change which panel you are on.

In closed position, if a new panel is clicked, it will automatically open.

Dropdown


Dropdown containers should always have a scrollbar inside. It should expand to the full width of the pizza if possible. Heighwise, it should extend to bottom of browser in order to show as much info as possible.

padding: 8px or 12px
